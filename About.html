Privacy Policy

At AplusITnHRConsultants, we are committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our employees, clients, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and disclose personal information in compliance with applicable laws, including the Indian Information Technology Act, 2000, and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for global data protection


1. Information Collection: We collect personal information, such as names, contact details, employment history, and educational qualifications, to facilitate HR processes, including recruitment, onboarding, payroll, and performance evaluation. We may also collect anonymous data for statistical analysis and website improvement.
2. Use of Personal Information: Personal information is used for legitimate HR purposes, such as assessing job applications, managing employment contracts, administering benefits, and providing professional development opportunities. We may also use aggregated data for internal reporting and analysis to enhance our HR practices.
3. Data Storage and Security: We implement reasonable security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Personal data is stored in secure servers, and access is restricted to authorized personnel only. We retain personal information for the duration required by law or for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected.
4. Data Sharing and Disclosure: We may share personal information with third-party service providers to perform HR-related functions, such as payroll processing, background checks, and training. These service providers are contractually obligated to handle personal data securely and in accordance with applicable data protection laws.
5. International Data Transfers: In cases where personal information is transferred outside India, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect the data. This includes implementing standard contractual clauses, obtaining necessary consents, or relying on adequacy decisions from relevant data protection authorities.
6. Individual Rights: Individuals have the right to access, rectify, or erase their personal information held by us. They can also withdraw consent, restrict processing, and object to automated decision-making. Requests regarding personal data should be submitted to our designated privacy contact, and we will respond in accordance with applicable laws.
7. Website and Cookies: Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, track website usage, and gather statistical data. Users can adjust their browser settings to manage cookie preferences.
8. Updates to the Privacy Policy: We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our HR practices, legal requirements, or technological advancements. Any updates will be posted on our website, and we encourage users to review the policy periodically.

By engaging with us or using our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you have any concerns or queries regarding the handling of personal information, please contact our designated privacy contact.
